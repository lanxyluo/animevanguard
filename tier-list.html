S+ tier highlights

Units listed here warp the meta and justify emergency pity planning. Expect nerf watch notes and upgrade priorities for each patch window.

Sukuna Secret — DPS

One of the strongest DPS units with 30% bonus damage against bosses. Peak DPS ceiling with strong AOE potential makes this the ultimate carry.

Pull priority: Ultimate • Best-in-slot DPS for endgame content.

Gojo Mythic — DPS

Top-tier burst/AOE DPS with insane damage output. Holy element cuts debuff duration by 50%, making him a late-game powerhouse.

Pull priority: Very High • Essential for boss burst strategies.

Igros & Alucard Secret — DPS

Elite secret units that dominate late game. Igros pairs perfectly with Song Jinwu for summoner comps, while Alucard excels at shredding bosses with bleed damage.

Pull priority: High • Specialized but extremely powerful.
