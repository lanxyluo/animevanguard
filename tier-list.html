Insane boss melting with the new Overdrive stacks. Pair with battery supports or stack cooldown reduction via Team Builder templates.

Mandatory haste aura for speed comps. Even at base investment it outperforms most reroll supports and accelerates wave clears.

Patch change log

Condensed notes from patch 1.11 so you know why the rankings shifted.

  • Sukura Ascendant +2% damage per Overdrive stack

    Reason for S+ bump — synergy breakpoints now easier to hit.

    Requires tighter positioning and dedicated stun support.

    Makes off-meta comps viable without double support slots.
